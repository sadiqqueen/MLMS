Who We Are

Vision & Mission

🔭 Our Vision

To be a leading national center for high-quality clinical training that produces competent, ethical, and patient-centered health professionals across all stages of medical and health education.

🎯 Our Mission
  • Integrate clinical training with academic curricula for all health and medical disciplines
  • Ensure structured, supervised, and competency-based clinical learning environments
  • Develop trainees' clinical skills, professionalism, communication and ethical behavior
  • Standardize and monitor clinical training according to national accreditation standards
  • Support continuous professional development of clinical trainers
